Last of the Snow Melt and Back to School!

Jan 22nd, 2010

Back in college again, and no doubt back to the weekly deadlines aswell! The first week has been relatively quiet but all the subjects that have had lectures so far seem like they will be quite Interesting. In particular, I’ll be having my first foray into assembly language and will be developing for a mobile phone for the first time.

Along with college I am doing my best to find myself a coding internship for the summer, so that I can get a real taste of what its like to work as a programmer.

Last weekend I got loads of paddling done. On Friday myself and Eoinor headed for Wicklow with a van and a bike, and we hit snow melt! We got the Dargle in at a sweet level and we also found time to fall down a medium/high Glenmacnass with snow covered rocks afterwards! Unfortunately Our crew of 2 didn’t include a photographer, so we only have this picture of the get on:

Then after missing out on the boating on Saturday, I got another 2 runs down the Dargle on Sunday, one of which was in a massive 13 person group!

Next weekend I am doing the Level 5 Skills Training course (water dependent), so I can start logging hours on these runs!
