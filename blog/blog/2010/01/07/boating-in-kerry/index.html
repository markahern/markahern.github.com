Boating in Kerry

Jan 7th, 2010

Had an awesome 3 days of kayaking in Kerry between Christmas and New Years. It was nice to get back in my boat after having to take a short break from paddling due to college. I have a few pics of the runs, courtesy of Kyle Tunney.

On the 29th December, myself and a few friends hit the Middle Roughty which had a healthy amount of water on it! I had done this river at a similar level 2 years ago, but I forgot just how many fun drops are on it! Everyone cruised down, bar the 2nd slot drop, which we all walked. Despite the freezing weather we had a blast!
